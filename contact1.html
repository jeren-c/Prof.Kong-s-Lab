Dr. Fanxin Kong

Professor at Syracuse University

Dr. Fanxin Kong is a tenure-track assistant professor in the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Syracuse University.

Personal Website

Interest: Cyber-Physical Systems and Internet-of-Things. (i) Security: attack detection and real-time recovery. (ii) Real-time and resource management: energy-efficiency, workload scheduling, and mechanism design. (iii) Intelligent CPS: trajectory planning and task allocation.

Applications: autonomous systems/vehicles, electric vehicles, and data centers.
